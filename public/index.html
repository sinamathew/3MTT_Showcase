Welcome!

I'm excited to share my learning journey in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI/ML) with 3MTT (Three Million Technical Talents) program. This page documents my experiences and discoveries as I delve into this fascinating field with the help of 3MTT's resources and training.

What is 3MTT?

The 3MTT program is a Nigerian government (FMCIDE) initiative aimed at training three million Nigerians in various technical skills over the next few years. This program seeks to equip Nigerians with the skills needed to thrive in the digital economy and position Nigeria as a leading exporter of tech talent.

While 3MTT offers training across various technical fields, my choosen track and focus here is AI/ML.

My Learning Process

My journey began with enrolment into the AI/ML program (Cohort 2), which grant me access to the community resources while waiting to be paired with a training provider.
